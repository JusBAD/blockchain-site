Who is BAD?

We are BAD (Blockchain At Davis)! We aim to create an environment for people interested in Blockchain to come together and expand their knowledge through workshops, collaborations on competitions and projects, and guest speaker events.

Learn More

Blockchain

A blockchain is a data structure that makes it possible to create a digital ledger of transactions and share it among a distributed network of computers. It uses cryptography to allow each participant on the network to manipulate the ledger in a secure way without the need for a central authority.

Who We Are

We're a student run organization at UC Davis. We provide Blockchain education and consulting services to the greater Sacramento region. Our members include alumni, students, and community members from all backgrounds. The team consists of undergraduate students from a variety of backgrounds: Material Science, Computer Science, Economics, and more. We strive to provide a supportive community through our weekly events that educate on Blockchain technology.
